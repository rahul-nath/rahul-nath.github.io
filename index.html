My Life Goal:

To build great things that bring us together as a society, towards an egalitarian future.

I am a Software Engineering Generalist with interests in Data Science. I'm currently looking for roles in Back-End/Full-Stack Development, having most recently working within a Flask/React stack in a microservices-based environment.

In my spare time I work on productivity apps that help me have more spare time, utilizing my skills with front-end, back-end, infrastructure, and data science. When I work on a project, I always try something new that I haven't before. This has led to learning platforms like React Native, Progressive Web Apps, Jupyter Notebooks, Pandas, Numpy/SciPy, sci-kit learn, Kubernetes, Docker, Redis, and more. Currently I'm working on improving an app I built for my father to keep track of his heart rejection medication.

My last role was as Technical Solutions Engineer at Google under a six-month contract. I want to eventually become a Full-Stack Data Engineer.

I like to generate novel solutions to real world problems, especially those of humanitarian nature. In my non-technical spare time I learn spoken languages (currently at Spanish, Bengali, and French) and practice meditation. I also DJ at venues around NYC and work on music production under the name shona. I'm also currently writing a novel (working title is "Our Home Was Seneca"). My work ethic is one of pushing forward in the face of discomfort and uncertainty, and getting over the fear of breaking things. I am a rebel by nature.
