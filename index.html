My Goal:

To create a lasting impact, or a useful service, for the betterment of society and our global communities.
Corny, but true.

Honing the skill set to do this technologically is only half the battle. Software development is no longer an impersonal art; it is an opportunity to understand and give back to society. To truly innovate, one needs to understand life through a variety of perspectives and draw from the diversity of one's experiences to make a lasting change.

While I practice the usual ways to bolster my technical skill set -- I do Project Euler problems, make and manage websites and apps -- I also enjoy unconventional activities like DJ'ing, weightlifting, and singing in an A capella group. I try different things, not just coding; my experiences help inform my problem solving abilities, and that's what sets me apart.
